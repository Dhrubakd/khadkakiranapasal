About Us

Welcome to Khadka Kirana Pasal, your trusted neighborhood grocery store that has been serving the community with dedication and care. We take pride in being more than just a store – we're your reliable partner in daily living.

Established with a vision to provide quality products at affordable prices, we have grown to become a household name in the locality. Our commitment to freshness, variety, and customer satisfaction sets us apart from the rest.

From fresh vegetables and fruits to daily essentials, personal care products, stationery, and household items – we stock everything you need under one roof. With over 191 carefully selected products across 18+ categories, we ensure you never have to go elsewhere for your daily needs.
